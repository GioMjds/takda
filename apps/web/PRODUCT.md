# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Business owners and staff in the informal service economy (e.g., public market/palengke stalls, barbershops, clinics, LGU offices) managing live queues, and storefront walk-in customers scanning QR codes on mobile devices to book same-day slots.

## Product Purpose

Provide a high-volume, low-friction queue and booking platform. Takda simplifies the appointment process into immediate same-day bookings, ensuring quick customer onboarding and real-time status updates for business operators.

## Brand Personality

Clean, organic, trustworthy, efficient, minimalist, modern, friendly, and approachable.

## Anti-references

- Over-decorated "ghost cards" (borders + heavy drop shadows).
- Saturated AI-cream backgrounds (#Bone, #Parchment, #Flour) used as a generic neutral.
- Sketchy SVG doodles, meta-criticism copy, or generic SaaS metrics dashboards.
- Over-rounded card borders (e.g., 24px+).

## Design Principles

- **Sunlight Legibility**: Maintain high contrast (minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio) for comfortable readability under bright outdoor sunlight.
- **Frictionless Action**: Prioritize speed, large touch targets, and clear input validation (specifically Philippine mobile numbers).
- **Restrained Visual Accent**: Use primary color (#1A8C75) and accent fill (#A8DDD4) deliberately to indicate interactive status, active selections, and state transitions, not for decorative backgrounds.
- **Light Footprint**: Maintain low bundle sizes and minimal client assets to ensure rapid loading on metered or slow mobile connections.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Strict WCAG 2.1 AA level contrast compliance.
- Support for `prefers-reduced-motion` with clean crossfades or instant state changes.
- Mobile-first layouts with minimum 48px touch targets for ease of use by busy business owners.
